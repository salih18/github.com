ATSEP Certification Studies
Air traffic safety electronics personnel (ATSEP) is an ICAO term for the technical staff involved with the creation and support of the ground-based electronic hardware and software systems used to support air navigation and Air Traffic Management. Often employed by Air Navigation Service Providers, ATSEPs are mainly engineers, technicians, hardware and software specialists who are responsible for the specification, procurement, installation, integration, calibration, maintenance, safety assurance and monitoring of these systems such as MSSR Radar Systems, ASMGCS, MLAT, SMR systems which are commonly used for Air Traffic in TURKEY. I attended a long certification process to responsible for being ATSEP.
